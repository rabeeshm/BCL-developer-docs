Brightcove Player Samples

This is a temporary STAGING document for review only. For the official Brightcove documentation site, go to support.brightcove.com

The following is a variety of code samples to show you some of the possibilities for customizing your Brightcove Player.

Background Video Random Playlist

In the player you will see videos randomly selected from a playlist that continually play as background videos. Also, at the start of each video the title and description of each video are displayed overlaying the background video.

Background Video Random Playlist

Displaying Ad Cue Points on Progress Bar

This sample shows a player where the three ad cue points associated with the video are displayed in the playback bar as blue markers. When you play the video, the ad will appear at 3, 10 and 17 seconds.

Displaying Ad Cue Points on Progress Bar

Ad Countdown Timer

For the pre-roll, mid-roll and post-roll ads, a countdown timer will appear in the top-left of the player.

Ad Countdown Timer

Age Gate

This sample displays an overlay and hides the player controls and play button. When the user confirms their age by clicking on the overlay text, the controls are displayed and the video plays.

Age Gate

Age Gate using ModalDialog

This sample uses the ModalDialog to initially cover the player. When the user confirms their age by clicking on the modal window, the controls are displayed and the video plays.

Age Gate using ModalDialog

Triggering Calls to Action (CTAs) with Cue Points

This sample shows two different CTAs displayed based on cue points. The first CTA is displayed at 3 seconds, for a duration of 5 seconds. The second CTA is displayed at 13 seconds for a duration of 3 seconds.

Triggering Calls to Action (CTAs) with Cue Points
