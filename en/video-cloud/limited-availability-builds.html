Brightcove Video Cloud SDKs (Limited Availability)

Note: the Android and iOS SDKs are currently in Limited Availability — if you wish to participate in the Limited Availability program, please contact your account manager. Note: the SDKs are not currently supported by Brightcove Support. You will receive support information when you join the Limited Availability program

Below are links to the current and legacy versions of the iOS and Android SDKs, and associated plugins. Note: if you are starting a new app or in the early stages of development, you should use the current version. Only use legacy versions when you have to for maintenance on an existing app.
