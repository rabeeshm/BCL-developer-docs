Sets DefaultHttpDataSource#DEFAULT_CONNECT_TIMEOUT_MILLIS as the connection timeout, DefaultHttpDataSource#DEFAULT_READ_TIMEOUT_MILLIS as the read timeout and disables cross-protocol redirects.
